Overview

Unique way to experience one of Africa's Great Lakes

Mantis Kivu Queen uBuranga is a one-of-a-kind luxury expedition vessel that allows you to explore the stunning African Great Lake, Lake Kivu. This magnificent lake forms Rwandaâ€™s western boundary and is renowned for its fishing industry, preserving the traditional methods of the Rwandan people. As you sail on either the 2 or 3-night itinerary, youâ€™ll be immersed in the splendour of the Kivu belt, filled with lush flora, diverse fauna, and enchanting nature trails.

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure as you embark on our carefully curated excursions. Traverse the hilly, subtropical terrain hiking, revealing endless natural wonders to captivate your senses. One of the highlights of the journey is the unique opportunity to connect with nature by visiting both chimpanzees in the Nyungwe Forest National Park and majestic gorillas in the Volcanoes National Park pre or post your sailing onboard the Kivu Queen.

Kivu Queen uBuranga sails on scheduled departures:

  • Saturdays sail south from Gisenyi sailing 2-nights to Nyamasheke
  • Tuesdays sail north from Nyamasheke sailing 3-nights to Gisenyi

Location

Mantis Kivu Queen uBuranga is situated on Lake Kivu between Nyungwe National Park and the Volcanoes National Park, offering a strategic base for exploring the wonders of the Kivu belt. With daily international flights landing in Kigali, Rwanda, and dependent on planned itinerary, guests have the option of either boarding the Mantis Kivu Queen uBuranga in the north or the south of Lake Kivu.

Kivu Queen scheduled itinerary:
